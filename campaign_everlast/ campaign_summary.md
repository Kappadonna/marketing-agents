# Campaign Summary for EverLast SPC

## Executive Summary
The EverLast SPC marketing campaign aimed to generate qualified leads from architects, interior designers, and B2B contractors focusing on sustainable flooring solutions. Through strategic iterations, we improved engagement and lead generation performance.

## Iterations Overview
### Iteration 1
**Performance Score**: 65/100  
- **Reach**: 15,000 impressions, 12,000 unique reach  
- **Engagement**: 300 likes, 45 comments  
- **Lead Generation**: 3 leads  
- **Key Learnings**: Need for enhanced interactive content and targeting for better conversion.  
- **Recommendations**: Adjust CTA strategies and improve lead magnets.

### Iteration 2
**Performance Score**: 75/100  
- **Reach**: 18,000 impressions, 15,300 unique reach  
- **Engagement**: 450 likes, 70 comments  
- **Lead Generation**: 5 leads  
- **Key Learnings**: Marked improvements in lead generation. Successful use of effective visuals and relevant content.  
- **Recommendations**: Introduce more interactive elements like polls; broaden strategies beyond LinkedIn.

## Performance Comparison
- **Iteration 1 vs Iteration 2 Performance**: Highlighted improvement in all significant metrics with a stronger focus on content quality and audience engagement.

## Best Performing Iteration
**Iteration 2** was the best performing due to improved audience engagement and lead generation, driven by refined content and targeted strategies.

## Conclusion
The campaign demonstrated that targeted content strategies and user engagement techniques significantly enhance lead generation capabilities. Future iterations should build on successful tactics while continuing to explore new interactive opportunities.

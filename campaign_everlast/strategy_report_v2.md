# Marketing Strategy Report for EverLast SPC - Iteration 2

## Executive Summary
The EverLast SPC flooring campaign aims to enhance lead generation among architects, interior designers, and B2B contractors engaged in premium commercial projects. Leveraging LinkedIn as the primary platform, this iteration focuses on refining our content strategy by increasing posting frequency and diversifying the types of content shared. Key insights gathered from the previous campaign reveal the need for more dynamic visuals and content formats like video, which can better capture the audience's attention and foster engagement. Furthermore, the campaign will align closely with the latest trends in the flooring industry, particularly the increasing demand for sustainable materials and innovative design.

## Target Audience Analysis
- **Demographics**: Aged 30-55, primarily architects and interior designers, property developers, hotel and retail managers, and flooring contractors who are active on LinkedIn.
- **Psychographics**: Motivated by aesthetic appeal, sustainability, innovation, and quality. They value partnerships with reliable suppliers and are keen on exploring sustainable building materials.
- **Behaviors**: Regularly engage with design-focused content on LinkedIn, prefer videos and case studies showcasing project successes, and often conduct thorough research before purchasing decisions.
- **Key Pain Points**: Difficulty in finding sustainable, high-quality flooring options that meet both aesthetic and functional requirements for premium projects.

## Positioning Strategy
- **Unique Value Proposition**: EverLast SPC offers a premium, sustainable flooring solution that combines aesthetic appeal with exceptional performance and durability, making it ideal for high-traffic commercial spaces.
- **Messaging Pillars**:
  1. Sustainability: Highlighting eco-friendly materials and recyclable options.
  2. Innovation: Showcasing advanced technology like the patented click-lock installation system.
  3. Aesthetics: Offering a diverse range of natural textures and designs.
  4. Durability: Emphasizing the wear resistance and moisture proof properties of the SPC material.
- **Tone and Voice Guidelines**: Professional yet approachable tone, with an emphasis on expertise in flooring solutions and a commitment to customer support.

## Content Strategy
- **Content Themes & Topics**: Sustainability in flooring, showcasing project case studies, tips on choosing the right flooring, and behind-the-scenes looks at the manufacturing process.
- **Storytelling Approach**: Emphasize real-life applications of EverLast SPC in commercial settings, including testimonials from satisfied architects and designers. Use visual storytelling to create an emotional connection.
- **Call-to-Action Strategy**: Encourage viewers to schedule consultations, request samples, and follow our page for more insights and updates.
- **Visual Style Recommendations**: Incorporate high-quality images and videos showcasing the flooring in various commercial spaces, with an emphasis on clean, modern aesthetics and vibrant colors.

## Expected Outcomes
- **Success Metrics**: Aim for a 30% increase in qualified leads, reaching over 65 lead conversions, and boosting engagement metrics across LinkedIn posts (likes, shares, comments) by at least 20%.
- **Predicted Engagement Levels**: Realistic expectations include increasing Impressions to at least 20,000 and a unique reach of 15,000 users, with content sharing experiences that resonate well with the target audience.

## Iteration Improvements
Based on the feedback from iteration 1, we will implement a more robust posting schedule to ensure consistent visibility. We will also diversify content types, focusing on engagement through videos and interactive posts, thereby enhancing follow-up strategies for leads generated.

## Conclusion
With a refined focus on sustainable flooring solutions and a dynamic content strategy aimed at architects and designers, iteration 2 of the EverLast SPC flooring campaign is poised for success. The integration of compelling visuals and diverse content formats will drive engagement, generate qualified leads, and solidify our standing in the competitive flooring market.
